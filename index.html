About Me

I am an Urban Planning graduate from Arizona State University with an interest in GIS, cartography, transportation planning, and environmental analysis. My work focuses on turning spatial data into clear visual stories that are useful for planning decisions.

I am building a GIS portfolio with projects that explore heat vulnerability, transit access, retail access, and neighborhood-level planning issues. I enjoy making maps that feel polished, simple, and easy to understand without losing the meaning behind the data.
